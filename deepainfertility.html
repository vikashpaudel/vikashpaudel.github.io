Specialized Services

Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive fertility evaluation, ovulation induction, IUI, and advanced reproductive health management for couples.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive gynecological surgeries including ovarian cyst removal, hysterectomy, and endometriosis treatment.

High-Risk Pregnancy

Expert management of high-risk pregnancies, including diabetes, hypertension, multiple gestations, and previous complications.

Menstrual Disorders

Treatment for PCOS, endometriosis, fibroids, abnormal bleeding, and hormonal imbalances.

Obstetric Care

Complete antenatal, intranatal, and postnatal care including normal delivery, C-section, and emergency obstetrics.

Family Planning

Contraceptive counseling, IUD insertion, sterilization, and safe abortion services as per national guidelines.
