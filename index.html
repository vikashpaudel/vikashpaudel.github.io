About Dr. Vikash Paudel

Dr. Vikash Paudel is a Consultant Dermatologist in Kathmandu, Nepal currently serving at Patan Hospital, Elite Health Clinic, and Shankhamul Healthcare. He is also an Associate Professor of Dermatology (TU), Patan Academy of Health Sciences (PAHS).

With special expertise in aesthetic dermatology and dermatosurgery, he is dedicated to advancing skin health and mentoring dermatology residents in clinical and research fields. Dr. Paudel has received international recognition, including the Rising Star Award at the World Congress of Dermatology (Milan 2019, Singapore 2023) and the Imrich Sarkany Award from the European Academy of Dermatology and Venereology (EADV, 2023).

Clinical Expertise

  • Acne, Pigmentation, and Skin Allergy Treatment
  • Hair Loss and Scalp Disorders
  • Aesthetic Dermatology (Fillers, Botox, Chemical Peels, Laser)
  • Dermatosurgery (Mole Removal, Scar Revision, Nail Surgery)
  • Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) & Genital Dermatology
