Professional Overview

Dr. Vikash Paudel is a highly qualified and experienced consultant dermatologist and venereologist practicing in Kathmandu, Nepal. With extensive training in both medical and aesthetic dermatology, Dr. Paudel brings a comprehensive approach to skin care, combining clinical expertise with the latest advancements in dermatological treatments.

Currently serving as a consultant dermatologist and associate professor, he is committed to providing accessible, high-quality dermatological care to patients across the Kathmandu valley (physical) and across different parts of the world (teledermatology).

Professional Philosophy

Dr. Paudel believes in a holistic approach to dermatological care, combining evidence-based medical treatments with personalized care plans.
